Principles

Paper over pixel

The canvas is warm off-white, not cold grey. Surfaces are distinguished by a hairline, never a drop-shadow.

One voice of accent

Crimson carries all primary actions and selection state. Amber, green and blue are reserved for status semantics only.

Small, honest data

A venue with ~6 shows a month doesn't need dashboards. Numbers are shown once, in serif, without sparklines.
