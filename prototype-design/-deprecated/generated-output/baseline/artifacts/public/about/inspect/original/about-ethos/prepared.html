Our ethos
01
Artists first
we exist to book weird shows and pay the people who play them. 100% of door to artists for all local shows under 100 cap.
02
A safer room
no racism, no transphobia, no bullshit. we enforce it. we mean it. the room has to be safe before it can be anything else.
03
Loud by design
the PA is tuned for real volume. we honor ear protection (free at the door) and we start on time. respect the room, respect each other.
